I'm a graduate Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering at Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), graduating in 2026. My work sits at the intersection of three worlds — VLSI and circuit design, machine learning, and software development — and I enjoy moving between them. On the hardware side, I design and verify circuits in Cadence Virtuoso using gpdk90 technology, running full DRC, LVS, and RCX flows. On the software and ML side, I build deep learning models with PyTorch and scikit-learn, handle data with the Python scientific stack, and ship web applications with PHP, React, and SQL. Beyond the technical, I've taken on leadership roles and earned recognition in national competitions. What sets me apart is range — I can take an idea from silicon to software and everything in between.
